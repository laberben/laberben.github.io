Group Meetings
All interested students are welcome to attend our weekly group meetings, held at 9 AM on Thursdays.

We are an Organometallic and Physical Inorganic Chemistry research group in the Department of Chemistry at the University of California and we address problems in the redox chemistry of biological and energy storage systems. We draw on expertise in fundamental synthetic organometallic and physical inorganic chemistry including the design, synthesis and characterization of new molecules. Our focus is primarily on the cheap, and earth abundant metals such as aluminum and iron.

We value a diverse range of backgrounds so that our team stays vibrant. Whatever their background, our lab members share a curiosity for making new molecules and discovering their electronic and reactivity properties. We welcome anyone who brings a passion for constantly learning new things and challenging themselves to think deeply and solve problems.
